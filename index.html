Theorem (Function rotation). Let $f$ be the function you want to rotate and $\theta$ be the angle counterclockwise from the $x$-axis you want to rotate it. Then the new function becomes $$y\cos \theta-x\sin \theta=f\left(x\cos \theta+y\cos \theta\right).$$
